## Idea 1: Cosmic Nebula Interface

**Design Movement:** Cosmic Minimalism meets Glassmorphism 2.0

**Core Principles:**
1. Deep space as canvas - dark backgrounds with subtle nebula gradients
2. Floating glass elements that feel weightless and ethereal
3. Light as information - glowing elements indicate interactivity
4. Organic motion - everything breathes and pulses subtly

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Deep space navy (#0a0f1a) - represents infinite possibility
- Accent: Cosmic teal (#00d4aa) - life, vitality, growth
- Secondary: Warm amber (#ffb347) - energy, nutrition, warmth
- Glass: White at 8-15% opacity with blur
- Emotion: Serene, futuristic, premium, trustworthy

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Floating card system - no rigid grids
- Z-axis depth with parallax scrolling
- Central focal points with orbital elements
- Asymmetric balance with visual weight distribution

**Signature Elements:**
1. Ingredient "molecules" - glowing spheres with inner light
2. Energy trails - particle effects following user interactions
3. Glass cards with frosted edges and subtle glow

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover reveals depth (scale + shadow increase)
- Click creates ripple/pulse effect
- Drag leaves luminous trail
- Success states explode into particles

**Animation:**
- Entrance: Fade up with slight scale (0.95 → 1)
- Hover: Gentle float + glow intensification
- Transitions: 400-600ms ease-out curves
- Ambient: Subtle breathing pulse on interactive elements

**Typography System:**
- Display: SF Pro Display (or Inter) - Bold 700 for headlines
- Body: SF Pro Text (or Inter) - Regular 400 for content
- Accent: Monospace for data/numbers
- Scale: 14px base, 1.5 line height, modular scale 1.25
</text>

## SELECTED APPROACH: Cosmic Nebula Interface

Am ales **Idea 1: Cosmic Nebula Interface** pentru că:
1. Se aliniază perfect cu viziunea "Spatial Food Computing" și estetica Apple VisionOS
2. Oferă cel mai bun echilibru între futurism și accesibilitate
3. Culorile calde (amber) pentru mâncare + reci (teal) pentru tech creează armonie
4. Glassmorphism-ul avansat este tendința 2025-2026
5. Permite animații fluide fără a fi copleșitor

### Design Tokens Finale:
- **Background:** Deep space gradient (#0a0f1a → #1a1f2e)
- **Glass:** rgba(255,255,255,0.08) cu backdrop-blur-xl
- **Primary Accent:** Cosmic Teal #00d4aa
- **Secondary Accent:** Warm Amber #ffb347
- **Text Primary:** White 90% opacity
- **Text Secondary:** White 60% opacity
- **Border:** White 10% opacity
- **Glow:** 0 0 20px rgba(0,212,170,0.3)
